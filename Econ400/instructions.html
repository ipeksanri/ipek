Instructions

Welcome and thank you for being voluntary in this experiment!

In this experiment, we expect you to make economic decisions. You earned 5 TL by showing up, and you can increase this number depending on your choices.

Your payment will be made according to your and others' decisions, after the experiment end.

The currency used in experiment is ECU, experimental currency rate. You will be informed about the ECU rate later.

In this experiment, there are 5 parts. The first 2 parts will be played before the game. You will begin with answering the comprehension questions. The second part is about guessing others' decisions. In the third part, you will play the game. Later, there is an exercise. And, finally, there is a questionnaire.

You will play the each part just one time.
